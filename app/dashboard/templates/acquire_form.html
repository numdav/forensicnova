Operational notes
  • Acquisition is synchronous. The pipeline runs libvirt dump, hashing, Swift upload and secure delete before returning.
  • Runtime scales with RAM: ~12 s for 512 MB, 2–3 minutes for 4 GB.
  • Do not close this tab during acquisition. If the browser times out, the pipeline still completes server-side and the new acquisition will appear in the list on refresh.
  • Integrity is verified end-to-end via Swift ETag comparison against local MD5.
